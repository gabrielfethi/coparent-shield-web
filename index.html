What It Does

Everything you need to
stay grounded and protected.

Six powerful tools designed specifically for parents navigating high-conflict co-parenting situations.

🔍

Message Analysis

Paste any message and instantly understand the manipulation tactics at play — guilt-tripping, gaslighting, DARVO, and more — with a severity score and plain-language explanation.

💬

Response Suggestions

Get strategically crafted Gray Rock, BIFF, and boundary-setting responses tailored to the specific message. Respond with calm instead of reaction.

👶

Child Scripts

Age-appropriate scripts for talking to your children about difficult co-parenting situations — without badmouthing the other parent or putting kids in the middle.

📓

Incident Journal

Document incidents in a structured, court-appropriate format. Factual, unemotional entries with timestamps — the kind attorneys and judges find credible.

📊

Pattern Reports

Analyze months of communication in one click. Identify escalation trends, trigger patterns, and recurring tactics — with clear language for your attorney.

🧭

Find Help

Curated directory of attorneys, therapists, and mediators who specialize in high-conflict custody situations — vetted for genuine expertise in this specific area.

How It Works

From message to clarity
in seconds.

No learning curve. No complex setup. Open the app when you need it.

1

Paste the message

Copy any text from iMessage, WhatsApp, email, or any other platform and paste it into the Analyze screen.

2

Get instant analysis

CoParent Shield identifies manipulation tactics, scores severity, and explains what's happening in clear, calm language.

3

Respond with confidence

Choose from AI-suggested responses designed to de-escalate, maintain boundaries, and protect your legal standing.
